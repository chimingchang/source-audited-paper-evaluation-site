This audit checks how much of the public ordering is point-estimate robust versus locally sensitive. It applies two small deterministic perturbations to the frozen v0.4.20 reports:

  • retrospective-weight shift: +/-0.10 within each report or claim maturity gate;
  • claim-weight transfer: up to 10% total score-bearing claim weight from the strongest claim to the weakest, and conversely.

These perturbations are not alternate rankings. They are a local sensitivity screen for cases that should not be overinterpreted as precise row-level orderings.
