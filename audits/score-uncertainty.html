This artifact adds interpretation bands around the frozen v0.4.19 point estimates. Bands are not alternate scores and are not written into the official ranking YAML. They are a public-reading guardrail against overinterpreting small score gaps.

Band Rule

Each score band is score +/- half_width, where half_width is the maximum of these transparent components:

ComponentRule
Local robustnessLargest point-score movement under retrospective-weight shift +/-0.10 and claim-weight transfer 10%.
Maturity floor0.4 mature validated, 1.2 active unresolved, 1.8 immature, 1.4 mature invalidated.
Claim-level floor0.6 for claim-level adjusted reports.
Cap floor1.5 when an invalidation cap is locally binding.
withheld calibration floor1.2 explicit private direction-risk, 0.8 score-bearing user counterfactual, 0.5 public-source-backed user confirmation.
Source floor0.3 for high-completeness/low-miss-risk rows, otherwise 1.0.

Adjacent row order is not treated as a precise public claim when the score gap is less than or equal to the sum of the two adjacent half-widths.
