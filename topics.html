How to read

Topics Are Lenses, Not Silos

Topic lenses are non-exclusive research-area views. A paper may appear in more than one lens when it functions as a bridge, predecessor control, or audit boundary across areas. They are topic-specific readings of the current source-audited sample, not field-complete rankings.
